How to Play Rules:

The Turtle code on the left display a certain shape! Try to guess what shape it will display!

If you guess wrong you loose 100 points.
If you guess right you gain 100 points.
Try to reach 500 points to win!

If you need help visualizing the drawing feel free to use the Sketch feature in the bottom right
of the window!
